FAST and AVOD Revamp Overview

  • Company: Plex
  • Platforms Covered: TV, iOS, Android, Responsive Web
  • Target Users: For FAST: General users (with and without accounts), PMS users with tuners. For AVOD: General, non-PMS users (with and without accounts)
  • Deliverables: Low fidelity wireframes created in Figma/Figjam, User research plans, synthesis reports, and recommendations, Competitive analyses across common features and functionalities within streaming services, UX analysis of current FAST and AVOD flows/metadata for mobile, web, and TV platforms, User personas creation for major user groups for FAST and AVOD users, Accessibility recommendations/considerations

Across the Streaming Content, Personal Media, and Playback product teams at Plex, I led all UX strategy and research efforts over the past couple years. One of the major projects for these teams started when the Streaming Content team was formed which was do an overhaul of the FAST and AVOD services on Plex.
