About Me

As a senior UX strategist, I focus on constructing the blueprint for user-centric features/products using research-based, user-informed approaches. I work cross-functionally with product, engineering, and product design/research leadership in order to dig deep into proposed problems, ensuring the correct problem is being targeted and developing the best strategic approaches and solutions to those key problems.

I have over 10 years of experience in user experience (UX), end-to-end product design, UX strategy/product strategy, user research, product management, and accessibility. I’ve had the privilege of working across multiple industries, from publishing to healthcare to consulting to fintech to film/entertainment. While wokring in each of these industries, I always focused on both deconstructing and constructing complex systems in order to best benefit both target users as well as product/company needs.

Outside of work, I have a very deep love and obsession of everything film, especially film history. I spent a good amount of my time exploring everything I can in film in addition to the hundreds of other hobbies I end up collecting. Currently, in addition to film, I’ve been completely consumed by learning about perfumes/fragrances, fitness, reading very outlandish books, and cooking/baking. If you ever need a walking dictionary of somewhat useless knowledge, send me a message and I will be more than happy to teach you everything I know.
