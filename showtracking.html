Show Tracking Overview

  • Company: Plex
  • Platforms Covered: TV, iOS, Android, Responsive Web
  • Target Users: General, non-PMS users that use multiple streaming services including Plex (PMS users were also considered, they just weren't the target audience)
  • Deliverables: Low fidelity wireframes created in Figma/Figjam, User research plans, synthesis reports, and recommendations, Usability testing around show tracking concepts to confirm placement, terminology, and flows, UX analysis of current Discover flows within Plex, Accessibility recommendations/considerations
